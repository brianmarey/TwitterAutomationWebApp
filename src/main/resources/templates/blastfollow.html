Follow Users

What Does This Do?

This tool lets you follow users en masse. Just enter a hashtag used by people on Twitter that you'd like to follow. Then, BlastFollow will automatically follow those users for you.

Be Careful

Twitter has been known to suspend accounts for aggressive following. Although BlastFollow will add several seconds between each follow, you should avoid following too many people in any one day. Carey Development, LLC assumes no responsibility for what happens to your account when you use this tool.

Pro-Tip

If you aren't sure how many people to follow, it's best to start small and then work your way up. Just follow 10 people today and come back tomorrow.

Let's Get Started
Enter a hashtag used by people you'd like to follow. For example, if you're into SEO, use the #seo hashtag. You don't have to enter the hashtag itself (but it's okay if you want to).
