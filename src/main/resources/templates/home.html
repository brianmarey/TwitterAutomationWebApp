How Does It Work?
It's Simple
Just login with Twitter (see above - and below). Then, create a project to track a variety of domains, subdomains, and URLs. You might like to track your own domain plus the domain of a few competitors in one project. Or, you might prefer to track a number of related URLs in a single project. It's up to you.

Once you've created a project, add a link. After a brief wait while our clowns gather important data from cyberspace, the app will treat you to numerous important statistics about the link you provided. We offer data on flow metrics, backlinks, keywords, and competitors.
