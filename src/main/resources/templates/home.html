How Does It Work?
It's Simple
You just provide a hashtag that's used by people who share your interests. For example, if you're into SEO, you might use the #seo or #contentmarketing hashtag. Once you've entered a hashtag, BlastFollow will let you know how many people it found who've tweeted with that hashtag. Then, you can automatically follow each one of them.

 

Keep In Mind
If you follow too aggressively, Twitter might suspend your account. It's best to follow only a reasonable amount of people in a given day. The point of this tool is to increase your productivity so that you don't have to manually follow people one-by-one.
